Software Testing basics

The Coderefinery project has given (in March 2021) a great workshop on software testing. Our focus is a bit different on the practical part, but for concepts and motivation, their material is great.

The material has an open license and can thus be freely used as basis for derivative work. We just link directly to the sections we want here, because they are great as it is.

  • Coderefinery’s motivations for testing

  • Coderefinery’s basic testing concepts

Before we meet online: Test design

Besides the concepts and motivation, Coderefinery has a good section about test design.

Since our workshop is based on hands-on tutoring, we ask you to read that section and try to apply the ideas to your project. Then on the online session we will follow up and discuss / help you with your problems.

  • Coderefinery’s section on test design

Maybe not all topics are applicable to your project (external dependencies, randomness), but try to apply those that are.
